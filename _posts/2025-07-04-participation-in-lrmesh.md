We have been working with others in the area to expand the coverage of the mesh network in central Arkansas and would like to invite you to participate.

Here is a description of [LRMesh](https://lrme.sh), be sure to visit the [website](https://lrme.sh) for more information.

LR Mesh is a community of individuals working to build a network of Meshtastic radios in the Central Arkansas area. This network acts like an area-wide text messaging system, allowing people to communicate publicly or privately with anyone on the network.
